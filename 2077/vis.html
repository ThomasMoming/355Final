CYBERPUNK2077

Cyberpunk 2077 Player Activity Data

The data in this project analyzes trends in Cyberpunk 2077 player activity, focusing on key events like DLC releases and spin-offs since the game’s launch. We gathered data mainly from SteamCharts, SteamDB, and gaming news sites, manually compiling it into a structured dataset for analysis.

Cyberpunk 2077 Player Distribution Map

Cyberpunk 2077 is an open-world role-playing game developed by CD Projekt Red and set in a futuristic city called Night City. Players take on the role of ‘V,’ a mercenary, and venture into a world of technophilia, social inequality, corporate domination, and hacker culture. The game's key features include rich character customisation, thrilling gun battles, a deep narrative, and reflections on a futuristic society, attracting many gamers into the sci-fi genre.
