Understand Cyberpunk2077 Right now?

So Why cyberpunk2077 is an anticipant game for player?

Cyberpunk 2077 appeals to users because of its unique futuristic perspective and deep immersive experience. It transports players into a world of technological advancement and social conflict, where every choice can change the course of the story. This combination of rich narrative and free play allows players to feel the full impact of their actions. The core appeal of the game lies in the detailed presentation of the futuristic city and the rich characterisation. Players can create unique adventures by customising their characters, skills and backgrounds. In addition, the game delves into themes of identity, free will , and the ethical challenges of technological advances that not only immerse players, but also provoke them to think about the real world.

Released in 2020, the game has undergone years of prior promotion and market building, and has successfully attracted a high level of anticipation from gamers around the world. Pre-orders reached a record 8 million copies, reflecting the huge interest in the game. Gamers are expecting a revolutionary open-world game, especially with CD Projekt Red's strong branding following the success of The Witcher 3.
The game's sales plummeted in 2021 because the game's many delayed releases wore down gamers' patience , causing some of them to begin questioning its quality. The game suffered from a number of technical problems after its release , especially its poor performance on previous generation consoles, which triggered widespread dissatisfaction among players. Word-of-mouth spread quickly, directly affecting subsequent sales performance.
The game's sales in 2022 are picking up as CD Projekt Red has invested heavily in fixing the game , releasing several updates to improve the player experience. As well as the successful launch of the Netflix animation Cyberpunk: Edgerunners , the game attracted a new player base and increased its market popularity. In 2023, the release of the DLC Phantom Liberty has injected new content into the game , attracting a large number of returning and new players. The quality of the game has improved after a number of fixes , attracting players who were initially disappointed with the game to give it a try again.

The rise in sales figures from 18 million to 20 million between 14 April 2022 and 28 September 2022 may be attributed to the launch of the Netflix animation Cyberpunk: Edgerunners. The success of the animation attracted a new demographic of gamers, rekindled interest in the game world, and led to a rebound in game sales. However, on the following 20 December 2022, it slipped to 13 million. This decline may be attributed to the game's early word-of-mouth being affected by technical issues , which despite being fixed by multiple updates, could not fully regain players' trust.

By 5 October 2023, sales figures had rebounded significantly to 25 million. This was largely due to the release of the DLC Phantom Liberty, an expansion that brought new storylines , characters, and gameplay mechanics to the game, further enticing existing and new players to purchase the game.

Why is The Witcher series so anticipated as CD Projekt Red's next step?
As the next step for CD Projekt Red, gamers‘ anticipation for the next instalment in The Witcher series stems from the stark contrast between The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt and Cyberpunk 2077’s tumultuous journey. The Witcher 3 won universal acclaim for its intricate storyline, memorable characters, and polished execution , while Cyberpunk 2077 was criticised for technical issues at its release, despite its ambitious narrative and world-building. This difference highlights CD Projekt Red's expertise in making immersive RPGs by focusing on their strengths , which was showcased in The Witcher 3. Additionally, since the upcoming Witcher game is being developed using Unreal Engine 5 , fans are excited about the game's new leap in graphical fidelity and realism , as well as the studio's signature storytelling approach. This return to the beloved world of The Witcher promises not only to be a polished and immersive experience , but also an opportunity for CD Projekt Red to re-establish its leadership in RPG development.
